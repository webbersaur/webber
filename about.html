About Us

Learn more about Webbersaurus and our mission to help local businesses thrive online.

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Webbersaurus has been helping Connecticut businesses establish their online presence for over 15 years. What started as a passion for web design has grown into a full-service web development company serving dozens of local businesses.

We believe every business deserves a website that works - one that's beautiful, easy to use, and helps convert visitors into customers. Our approach combines creative design with practical functionality.

Based in Branford, CT, we're proud members of the Shoreline Chamber of Commerce and deeply committed to supporting our local business community.

Why the Dinosaur?

You might be wondering about our prehistoric mascot. The dinosaur represents strength, longevity, and the fact that great design never goes extinct. Plus, it's memorable - and in the web world, standing out matters!
