Our Story

Founded in 2007, Webbersaurus has been helping businesses establish their online presence for over 18 years. What started as a passion for web design has grown into a full-service web development company serving dozens of local businesses.

We believe every business deserves a website that works - one that's beautiful, easy to use, and helps convert visitors into customers. Our approach combines creative design with practical functionality.

Based in Branford, CT, we're proud members of the Shoreline Chamber of Commerce and deeply committed to supporting our local business community.
